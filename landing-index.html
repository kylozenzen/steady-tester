The Steady philosophy
01

Science, not fads.

BMR + Mifflin-St Jeor formulas using your actual stats — height, weight, activity, training frequency. No generic 2,000 calorie defaults. Math that works for you.

02

Your data is yours. Period.

No server. No cloud. No account. Your food log and targets live in your browser's local storage. We couldn't access your data even if we wanted to.

03

Installs like a real app.

Steady is a PWA. Add it to your home screen on iOS or Android — opens instantly, works offline, bypasses the App Store. No download, no update prompts.

04

Consistency > perfection.

Streaks, weekly averages, and gentle insight cards keep you honest without the shame spiral. One bad day is just data — it doesn't erase the week.

Most fitness apps treat you as the product. Steady treats your privacy as a feature.
